Clever Matching with Clovr

Welcome to Clovr, a social networking tool and event for UNC CS students. Here, you can fill out a questionnaire detailing your interests and hobbies, and be matched with other students like you via our algorithm. Your matches will be revealed on a fun night of chatting, speed-dating style virtual social event on March 17th at 7:00pm EST.

The Zoom Link will be emailed to you upon submitting the questionnaire.
