About Clovr

Clovr is a social networking event for CS students, born of the need for building new connections during COVID-19. It is intended as a welcoming space for students to make friends, meet people with similar interests, and take time to destress from school. Participants complete a questionnaire and receive matches with similar interests via our algorithm, with the event culminating in a Zoom meetup where matches are revealed.

We ask that everyone must be respectful to other participants and also understand and agree to our Terms and Conditions before participating.
